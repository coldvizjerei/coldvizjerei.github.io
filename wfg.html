The life license is the first step

  1. Study online (ExamFX) to get the pre-licensing certificate(s)!
    1. Go to mywfg.com and login to your account
    2. Go to Tools --> Licensing & Training --> Pre-licensing Education --> ExamFX
    3. Click on New Users: Sign up for ExamFX --> Continue --> New Student --> Life and Health Insurance --> New Jersey --> ExamFX Flex Package ($79.95)
    4. Finish the registration/payment process and start studying! (Beginning with life)
      • Study by topic
      • Simulate your exam (70% to pass)
      • Guarantee Exam (80% to pass)
    5. Print your Pre-Licensing Certificate(s) of Completion

  2. Schedule and take your licensing exam(s) through PSI Exams
    1. Create an account
    2. Register for an examination
        3. Use the following info:
      • Organization: Government/State Licensing Agencies
      • Jurisdiction: New Jersey
      • Account: NJ Life Producer (or NJ Accident and Health Producer)
      • School/Provider: ExamFX
    3. Schedule your examination date(s)/location(s)
    4. Make sure to bring your prelicensing certificat and 2 forms of ID! (forms of ID include Driver's License and Credit Card)
    • General Tips/Notes
      • ExamFx is an online study sitel once you sign up, you have a 3 month time window for you to study and pass the online exam to get the prelicensing certificate
      • You will pass the online test (and the real test) if you score 70% or above. Therefore, once you are scoring consistently above 80%, you should be ready to take the real thing!
      • All questions are multiple choice. It's recommended to take the end of chapter and practice quizzes repeatedly to familiarize yourself and understand the test questions/answers. (Quizzing helps retain knowledge + its also less boring!)
      • You can pick your test date before you finish obtaining the certificate of completion. As long as you get the certificate in time for the test, it's all good!
      • Study for and pass the life exam first, then the accident and health one next!

  3. Get your fingerprints
    1. Fill out this Universal Fingerprint Form
    2. Go to bioapplicant.com --> Start Here
        3. Use the info provided below:
      • Originating Agency Number: NJ920560Z - DEPT OF INSURANCE
      • Category: INK
      • Statute/Reason for Printing: 17:22A-32 INSURANCE PRODUCER'S LICENSE
      • Document Type: RB1
      • Contributor's Case Number: INSPRODUCER
    3. Complete registration and scheduling process
    4. Bring Universal Fingerprint Form and a form of Acceptable ID
        5. Examples of Acceptable ID:
      • Valid U.S. State Photo Driver's License/Non Driver's License
      • U.S. Passport

  4. Apply for new resident license online
    1. Go to nipr.com --> Begin Application (located on the sidebar) --> Accept
        2. Use the info provided below:
      • Resident State: NJ
      • License Type: Individual
      • What do you want to do: Apply for a new Resident License, Add a Line of Authority, OR Resume an existing application
      • Enter SSN & Last Name
      • Click Next
      • Click Next
      • Select Line of Authority --> Life
      • Select Line of Authority --> Accident & Health Or Sickness
      • Click Next
      • State Question --> Select No (not a disabled veteran)
      • Personal Data --> Fill out the information
      • Business Data --> 666 Plainsboro Rd, Suite 1022, Plainsboro NJ 08536
      • Mailing Address --> Use Residential Address
      • Aliases --> None
      • Affiliations --> None
      • Employment History --> Fill out
      • Background --> Answer the Questions
      • Attestation --> Agree
      • Verification Email --> Enter all emails
      • Submitter --> Yourself
      • Submit payment of fees, and print receipts of form and payment
      • Wait for transaction approval

  5. Register and print your new license!

  6. Make sure your Senior Marketing Director (SMD) or leader adds you to the licensed people email lists

Congratulations! You now have your license! Next (and last) step is to complete a couple easy online studies and get appointed with various companies so you can get paid for referrals and services!
